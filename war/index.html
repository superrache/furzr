About this app

furzr offers an original way to explore the world of music, browsing relationships between artists or bands and listening samples. This app lets you expand your musical knowledge like the leaves of a tree and find out what you will be listening soon.
furzr uses data from last.fm, 7digital and Rovi.

Any suggestions, questions and comments are welcome
